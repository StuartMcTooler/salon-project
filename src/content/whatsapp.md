# How Bookd uses WhatsApp

Bookd uses **WhatsApp Business** to send booking communications between independent barbers and their clients. We chose WhatsApp specifically because it supports the rich, transactional messages a haircut appointment benefits from — most importantly, a photo of the client's previous cut, pulled from the barber's Look Book.

This page describes exactly what we send, how clients opt in, and how clients can opt out. It is intended to be transparent for clients, barbers, and platform reviewers.

## What we send via WhatsApp

When a client books with a Bookd barber, we send the following messages on the barber's behalf:

- **Booking confirmation.** Appointment time, location, price, and where the client has consented, the client's previous haircut photo from the barber's Look Book. Confirmation messages are sent immediately on booking.
- **Appointment reminder.** Sent 24 hours and 2 hours before the appointment, with a link to reschedule.
- **Rebook nudge.** Sent at an appropriate interval after the appointment, with a one-tap rebook link.

We do **not** use WhatsApp for promotional marketing, sale of unrelated goods or services, or any purpose outside the booking relationship between the client and the barber.

## How clients opt in

Clients consent to WhatsApp messaging at the point of booking, when they confirm the phone number they wish booking communications to be sent to. The Bookd booking form makes clear that the confirmation will arrive via WhatsApp (with SMS as a fallback for clients who don't use WhatsApp).

For the Look Book photo specifically, the client provides separate consent through the in-app image consent flow at the time the photo is captured by the barber.

## How clients opt out

Clients may opt out of WhatsApp messages from Bookd at any time, by any of the following methods:

- Replying **STOP** to any Bookd WhatsApp message
- Asking the barber to disable WhatsApp on their client record
- Emailing **support@bookd.ie**

Clients who opt out, or who don't have WhatsApp installed, continue to receive the same booking communications via **SMS**, so that no client misses their confirmation.

## Privacy

Our handling of personal data is described in our [Privacy Policy](/privacy). WhatsApp messages are processed by **Meta Platforms Ireland Limited** as a processor on our behalf, under Meta's WhatsApp Business Terms and Data Processing Terms. We do not share message content with anyone outside the processors needed to deliver the message.

## SMS fallback

For clients without WhatsApp, the same booking confirmations, reminders, and rebook nudges are sent over **SMS** via **Twilio**. The SMS version omits rich content such as the Look Book photo.
