# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

## Who we are

Bookd ("Bookd," "we," "us," or "our") is operated by **Downthesofa Ireland Limited**, a company registered in Ireland with company registration number **538446** and registered office at **17 Northbrook Terrace, North Strand, Dublin, D03WV44, Ireland**.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) ("GDPR") and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018, Downthesofa Ireland Limited is the data controller of the personal data processed in connection with the Bookd service ("Service").

For any data protection question, email **support@bookd.ie**.

## Who this policy applies to

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- **Barbers** — independent barbers and barbershops who subscribe to Bookd Pro and use the Service to manage their bookings, clients, payments, and customer records.
- **Clients** — end customers of those barbers, whose details are recorded in Bookd and who may receive booking-related communications from us on the barber's behalf.

It also applies to visitors to bookd.ie.

## What personal data we collect

**Information you give us when you register as a Barber:**

- Name, business or trading name, phone number, and email address
- Working hours, services offered, pricing, location, and deposit rules
- Payment account information collected by our payment processor (see "Sharing data" below)
- Photos and notes you upload to the Look Book feature, voice notes recorded after appointments

**Information about Clients, entered by Barbers or provided during the booking flow:**

- Client name and phone number
- Appointment history and preferences
- Photos taken by the Barber for the Look Book, where the Client has consented under our in-app image consent flow
- Voice notes the Barber records after an appointment, transcribed and saved against the Client record
- Payment details collected by our payment processor at the time of booking

**Information collected automatically when you use the Service:**

- Device type, browser, operating system, and basic usage data
- IP address and approximate location, for security and fraud prevention
- Log data such as access times and pages viewed

## Why we collect it (lawful basis under GDPR)

We process personal data on the following lawful bases:

- **Performance of a contract** — to provide the Bookd Service to Barbers under the Bookd Pro Terms, and to process bookings and payments for Clients.
- **Consent** — for the use of Client photos in the Look Book feature, and for any non-essential cookies or marketing communications.
- **Legitimate interests** — to improve and secure the Service, to prevent fraud, and to send transactional booking communications to Clients that the Barber has arranged on the Client's behalf.
- **Legal obligation** — to comply with accounting, tax, and other regulatory requirements.

## How we use WhatsApp and SMS

Bookd sends booking-related messages — booking confirmations, appointment reminders, and rebook nudges — to Clients on behalf of the Barber. Messages are sent via WhatsApp Business where the Client uses WhatsApp, and via SMS otherwise.

**WhatsApp Business messages** may include the Client's previous haircut photo from the Barber's Look Book, where the Client has consented to its use under the in-app image consent flow. When messages are sent via WhatsApp Business, **Meta Platforms Ireland Limited** (WhatsApp's EEA operator) processes the message content and metadata as a processor on our behalf, in accordance with Meta's WhatsApp Business Terms and the WhatsApp Business Data Processing Terms.

**SMS messages** are sent via **Twilio Inc.**, which processes the message content and metadata as a processor on our behalf.

Clients may opt out of WhatsApp or SMS messages at any time by replying STOP, by asking the Barber to disable messaging on their record, or by emailing support@bookd.ie.

## Who we share personal data with

We share personal data only with the categories of recipient below, under appropriate written data processing agreements:

- **Stripe Payments Europe Limited** — for processing card payments, Tap to Pay transactions, and same-day payouts to Barbers.
- **Meta Platforms Ireland Limited (WhatsApp)** — for sending WhatsApp Business messages to Clients.
- **Twilio Inc.** — for sending SMS messages and managing related telephony.
- **Cloud hosting providers** — for hosting the Service infrastructure.
- **Professional advisors** — accountants, lawyers, and auditors, where strictly necessary.
- **Regulators, courts, and law enforcement** — where required by law or to protect our rights.

We do not sell personal data, and we do not share personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## International transfers

Some of our processors (notably Meta, Stripe, Twilio, and certain hosting providers) may transfer personal data outside the European Economic Area. Where this occurs, transfers rely on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses, an adequacy decision, or another lawful transfer mechanism, with supplementary safeguards as appropriate. A copy of the relevant safeguards can be requested at support@bookd.ie.

## How long we keep data

- **Barber account data** — for the duration of the subscription, plus up to 7 years afterwards for accounting and tax purposes.
- **Client booking history** — for the duration of the Barber's subscription, plus up to 7 years where required for accounting and tax compliance.
- **Look Book photos and voice notes** — for the duration of the Barber's subscription, or until the Client withdraws consent, whichever is sooner.
- **WhatsApp and SMS message logs** — 12 months.
- **Website analytics and security logs** — 13 months.

Where you close your account, we will retain only the data we are required to keep by law.

## Your rights under GDPR

Where we process your personal data, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Have inaccurate data corrected
- Have your data erased (subject to legal exceptions)
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability — receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
- Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing)
- Lodge a complaint with the **Data Protection Commission of Ireland** at dataprotection.ie

To exercise any of these rights, email **support@bookd.ie**. We will respond within one month.

If you are a Client, the Barber you have booked with is the primary point of contact for many of these requests. You may direct requests to us at any time and we will route them appropriately.

## Data security

We use industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including encryption in transit, role-based access controls, regular security review, and Stripe's PCI-DSS compliant payment infrastructure. No system is perfectly secure, but we take security seriously and will notify affected users and the Data Protection Commission in the event of a personal data breach, in line with our legal obligations.

## Cookies

We use a small number of strictly necessary cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your preferences. We use analytics cookies only with your consent, which you can give or withdraw at the cookie banner.

## Children

The Bookd Service is intended for use by Barbers aged 18 or over. Clients booking through Bookd should be of an age appropriate to make a barbering appointment under applicable law. We do not knowingly process the personal data of children under 16 without a parent's consent.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified to Barbers by email at least 30 days before they take effect, and to Clients at the next booking communication.
